blog, in full Web log or Weblog, online journal where an individual, group,
corporation presents a record of activities, thoughts, or beliefs. Some blogs operate mainly as news filters,
collecting various online sources and adding short comments and Internet links.

A blog (a truncation of "weblog") is an informational website published on the World Wide Web consisting of discrete,
often informal diary-style text entries (posts). Posts are typically displayed in reverse chronological order so
that the most recent post appears first, at the top of the web page.
